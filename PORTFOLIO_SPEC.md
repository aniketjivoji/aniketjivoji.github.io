# PORTFOLIO SPECIFICATION

---

# Objective

Build a modern engineering portfolio for **Aniket Jivoji**, a Mechanical Design Engineer.

The portfolio is intended to secure Mechanical Design Engineer, Product Development Engineer, Medical Device Engineer, Robotics Engineer, and Aerospace Design Engineer roles.

The portfolio should demonstrate engineering thinking, structured product development, and real-world design experience rather than simply presenting projects.

---

# Design Philosophy

The portfolio should feel:

- Minimal
- Premium
- Engineering focused
- Clean
- Professional
- Fast
- Easy to scan

Avoid unnecessary animations, visual clutter, gradients, decorative elements, or gimmicks.

The portfolio should look like something created by an engineering consultancy rather than a personal blog.

---

# Design Language

Use the HTML5 UP Story template as the base.

Preserve:

- Typography
- Responsiveness
- Scroll animations
- General spacing
- Story visual identity

Only modify sections where necessary.

Do not redesign the template.
